The Problem: Carbon Sequestration is a Cost Center

Current carbon capture technology is a financial black hole. Companies spend billions to pump CO2 underground, hoping it stays there. There is no ROI. There is no product. There is only "less bad." This model is a failure of imagination. To solve the climate crisis, we must stop treating carbon as waste and start treating it as the ultimate raw material.

The Solution: The SkyForge "Atmospheric Weaver"

We don't just capture carbon. We forge it. Vertex Carbon Systems has developed a proprietary Aero-Polymerization process. Our modular "Weaver" units pull CO2 directly from the atmosphere and catalyze it into high-tensile, structural-grade carbon fiber.

We are turning the sky into a mine for the 21st century's most valuable material.

* Active Capture: Each unit removes 500 tons of CO2 annually.
* Net-Negative Output: For every kilogram of fiber produced, we sequester three kilograms of atmospheric carbon.
* Zero Waste: Our only byproduct is pure oxygen.
